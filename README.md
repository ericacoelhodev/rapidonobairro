# rapidonobairro
A window of neighborhood shops and services.
www.rapidonobairro.com.br

We are showing to the neighbors the stores there are near them anda they don't now. 
With this  we hope to increase the sales and comunication between them.

In this app we have the store ou service information, contact in a landpage which shows theire products, chat, calender, scheduling, cupons of discount, loyalty cards with rewards, shopping carts...
There's also an page of news of the neighborhood with donations, and useful phones like emergency or police for exemple. Some of the social works are here too, so people can donate ou works voluntary to help people near by.
